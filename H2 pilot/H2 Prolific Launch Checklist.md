# H2 Prolific Launch Checklist

## Pilot target

- Pilot N = 60
- Confirmatory N = 300 recruited, N ≥ 260 analyzable
- Age 18+
- Fluent English
- Country: US / UK / CA / AU
- Time estimate: 8–12 minutes
- Compensation: at least $12/hr equivalent

## Must check before launch

- H2 prereg locked on OSF before confirmatory collection
- Pilot data labeled exploratory and not pooled
- Use `H2_vignette_stimuli_v3.csv`
- Confirm condition is between-subjects
- Confirm each participant sees 4 vignettes, 1 per domain
- Confirm allocation counts are logged

## Exclusions

- Completion time under 2 minutes
- Failed 2/2 attention checks
- Straight-lining: SD = 0 across all DVs for at least 3 vignettes

# H3 Composure Credibility Asymmetry – Protocol

## Purpose

Test whether polished therapeutic presentation beats distressed evidence-rich presentation in institutional-style review.

## Design

2×2 between-subjects:
- Evidence strength: strong vs weak
- Tone: calm/therapeutic vs distressed/blunt

N ≈ 160–200.

## Primary DV

Credibility judgment.

## Hypothesis

Tone-polished harmful messages will be judged as more credible than distressed blunt messages, even when the distressed party has stronger evidence.
